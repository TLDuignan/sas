About Us

Parks are the heart of a community. Parks provide opportunities for living, learning, and leading a full and productive life. Southridge Park is over 20 acres of beachside greenways, alethic fields and courts, picnic areas, playgrounds, and a recreation center. We have many clubs and activities for those of all ages. Go for a lovely evening nature walk or grab some friends and play a game of basketball. From the casual athlete to the nature enthusiasts, Southridge park has something for everyone.
